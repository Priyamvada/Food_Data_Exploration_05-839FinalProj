Food Waste

The clock is ticking

"127.6 billion dollars is the value of food wasted by Americans every year"

74.6 billion dollar is the value of food stamps issued every year by the US government.

Worldwide, land the size of 1 and a half USA is used to grow food, livestock or dairy production that no one would eat.

If food waste was a country it would be the third biggest greenhouse gas emitter after US and China.

Water lost in growing food can be used by households of US, Canada and Mexico combined for a year.
